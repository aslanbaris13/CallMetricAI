## Overview

CallMetricAI is a Multimodal Agentic AI platform that analyzes call center interactions through speech, text, and emotion layers. The system provides real-time coaching for agents and actionable dashboards for managers — reducing operational costs by **30%** while improving decision-making speed.

Developed at **Bilsoft Software**, hosted at [Düzce Teknopark](https://callmetricai.com).

## What It Does

CallMetricAI automates call center quality assurance by processing audio and text data through AI pipelines. The platform delivers real-time agent assistance during calls and provides managers with performance dashboards and coaching insights.

Key capabilities (as publicly described on [callmetricai.com](https://callmetricai.com)):

- 🎤 **Speech-to-Text** — Automatic transcription of call center audio
- 😤 **Emotion & Sentiment Analysis** — Real-time detection of stress, empathy, and satisfaction
- 🎯 **Intent Recognition** — Automated extraction of customer intent from conversations
- 🤖 **Real-Time Agent Assist** — Live suggestions for agents during active calls
- 📊 **Manager Dashboard** — KPI tracking, performance metrics, and coaching reports
- 🔒 **KVKK Compliant** — Data anonymization and role-based access control
